How can the data be used practically and in a sustainable way? Here we outline pathways for policymakers and journalists to strengthen climate change media coverage in Flanders. For Policymakers

Use media analysis to design more effective climate communication strategies and regulatory frameworks.

  • policy Strengthen media guidelines: Encourage balanced and evidence-based reporting through updated broadcasting standards.
  • groups Support public broadcasters: Invest in explanatory journalism and science communication.
  • gavel Align regulation across regions: Compare Flemish and Walloon frameworks to identify gaps and best practices.

For Journalists

Leverage insights to improve accuracy, framing, and depth in climate reporting.

  • school Training & literacy: Develop newsroom training on climate science and data interpretation.
  • fact_check Recognise misinformation patterns: Learn to recognise misinformation patterns in media and employ this knowledge in your work.
  • balance Improve framing: Move beyond scaring narratives toward solution-oriented coverage.
  • public Diversify sources: Include scientists, local voices, and affected communities.
